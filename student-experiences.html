They’ve regularly organised various workshops and competitions in order to help us achieve all-round development. Though I belong to a core department, we were given the advantage of CRT classes from the beginning of 3rd year itself along with sessions on mock interviews and special coding classes. I would like to thank each and every faculty who helped me and my special thanks to the training and placement cell of our college.

Ashwith Sreepathi
B.Tech - ECE (2017-2021)

DVR & Dr. HS MIC College of Technology is a practical technical institute and offers an ideal study environment to those who want to grow both professionally and educationally. In 2021, I graduated from MICT with Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communications with placement offers in India's top 2 MNCs - Infosys as System Engineer and Accenture as an Associate Software Engineer. Through my education and experience in various activities held in the campus premises, I realized what I want to do in the future – to become a Scientist. I realized that both professional experience and higher education are important to achieve my future goal. I was guided by faculty who were best of their class and they helped me to reach my goal. In addition, the impact of the events we've organized in our college like TechnoMIC 2019, Pep Talks etc., helped me to draw my hidden ability of leadership, management quality and team player skills. The journey with MICT will become a dream to every student who has the thirst for knowledge and willingness to excel in the respective technical domains.


Naga Siva Kyathi. A
B.Tech - ECE (2017-2021)

I was really excited to study at MICT. The infrastructure and huge library with a plethora of books are the best aspects of MICT. ECE department has good lab facilities and through these labs, we have gained enough practical exposure besides theoretical knowledge. The professors are very supportive and are always ready to help us solve our problems. In addition, the college gave me a lot of opportunities. I was an acting Vice-president of E-CLUB in our college and hosted several programs in our college. The TEP program by the Indian School of Business helped us a lot. It’s a great environment to make new friends. A lot of training was provided for our placements covering every small detail like building our resume to conducting mock interview sessions which helped me to get placed in TCS and Infosys. I would recommend MICT for any self motivated student. As a student I cherish these moments and I feel blessed to be a part of MICT.


Gopi Krishna
B.Tech - ME (2017-2021)

Gold medal in high jump
Winner of JNTUK Badminton, JNTUK Zone ‘C’ sports meet.

Sports are highly encouraged at MIC. I would like to say that the credit for all my achievements undoubtedly goes to MIC college. The physical education department has identified my special talent and supported me by giving coaching at different levels. The physical education department has specially developed a sports environment that ignites the spark in every sports person. 400 mts standard athletics track, spacious grounds for cricket, badminton, volleyball, basketball, throw ball kabaddi, kho-kho, and separate rooms for indoor games like table tennis, caroms, chess etc encourage the sporting spirit in every student. The college has conducted JNTUK zone ‘C’, JNTUK zone ’c’ cricket tournaments and various other national, state and district events and tournaments. Conducting such events really motivate many students to participate in sports. I specially would like to state that identifying my skill set, the college management has given me a free seat in B. Tech. This shows how sports are encouraged at MIC College. Thank you!
